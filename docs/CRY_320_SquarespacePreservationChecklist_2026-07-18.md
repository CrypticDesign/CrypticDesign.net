## Required evidence packet

- Route inventory: every page, article, category/tag archive, product/store URL, policy page, system/login URL, and current HTTP status.
- Content export: page/article copy, titles, slugs, publish/update dates, authors, excerpts, credits, and rights disposition.
- Media manifest: source URL, filename, type, dimensions, caption, alt text, owning page, rights status, local archive path, and checksum.
- Metadata capture: title, description, canonical, Open Graph fields/image, social-card fields, structured data, and index/no-index state.
- Forms: field labels, required state, consent language, destination address/workflow, autoresponder behavior, and screenshot. Do not submit test data without approval.
- Commerce: store/category/product URLs, product copy, media, variants, prices observed, fulfillment integration, and disposition. Do not change orders, inventory, billing, or products.
- Analytics/cookies: provider names, property/container identifiers without secrets, consent configuration, and preservation/retirement decision.
- Redirects: Squarespace-managed redirects plus the approved legacy-to-v18 map, with status-code and loop checks.
- DNS/TLS: root, `www`, MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC, verification records, nameservers, certificate state, and dated screenshots. Do not modify records.
- Visual record: desktop and mobile full-page screenshots of every public route and representative article/product states.
- Account export: available Squarespace exports, export date, limitations, archive location, and checksum manifest.
- Recovery proof: current subscription remains active, existing site remains reachable, and rollback owner/action is named.
